About Us

Ace the Academia is a South African-based education company founded in 2022. Our vision is to empower South Africans through education to improve their living standards. We started our journey working online teaching grade 10, 11, and 12 using Google Meet and Zoom.

At Ace the Academia, we believe in growth, understanding, and knowledge. We understand that by understanding people, they can be established, and by knowledge, they can fill every area of their lives with all precious and pleasant riches. We are highly involved in helping students at an individual level to be established and have a great life filled with all good things.

Our Services

Tutoring

We offer one-on-one and group tutoring services for various subjects to help students achieve their academic goals.
we offer fremium plan where we help our students with their homeworks and questions they might have on whatsapp
and premium plan where we offer online lessons on google meet and zoom and physical lessons at university of the Western Cape

Mentorship

We have a mentorship program that matches students with mentors in the industry to guide and support them in their career aspirations.

Career Guidance

We provide career guidance services to help students make informed decisions about their future careers.
